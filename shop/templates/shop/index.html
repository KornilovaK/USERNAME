ABOUT

CARE is our first project, our inspiration and our lifelong love.

Before opening, we studied for a long time, traveled to other cities, met baristas from eminent coffee houses, and gained experience. Then it took a long time to choose a roaster for our coffee and a supplier of real fresh tea. After some time, we were lucky and we settled on our partners with whom we work to this day.

MENU

The menu of the coffee house has a large selection of coffee and tea, various desserts, as well as freshly squeezed juices and sandwiches. The latest addition to the menu is waffles. We became addicted to them after trying them in Brussels while on vacation.
